Meet the Racers

Taylor Barnett



Bio

Born: August 19, 1997

I am 16 years old, born in Hodges, S.C. to Stacy and Sharon Barnett. I have 1 brother Chandler. I have been riding motorcycles since the age of 3 and have been racing since age 5. I am currently in the 11th grade at Greenwood High School in Greenwood, S.C. I am an honor roll student and place an emphasis on my education.

I'm currently riding a 2014 Kawasaki in four different classes:

250B

450B

250B Mod

Schoolboy 2

I have a great amount of passion for this sport and am currently qualifying locally and regionally. My long term goal for this year is to race in the 2014 Loretta Lynn AMA Nationals.

I would like to thank Mumz Racing for giving me this opportunity to ride for such an amazing team. I will always give 100% and strive to be the best in this awesome sport.

Justin Gonzales



Bio

Born: September 5, 2002

I am 11 years old, born in Weare, N.H. to Peter and Heidi Gonzales. I have 1 brother Johnathon. I began racing at age 4 on a PW 50 and have for the last 6 years have worked my way up starting at Novice levels through Amateur classes on 50's and 65's. I am currently in the 6th grade at Weare Middle School in Weare, N. H. I am an honor roll student and place an emphasis on my education.

I am riding a RM85 Suzuki in the following two classes:

85B

Super-mini

My goal for 2014 is to attain a top 5 in 85B and Super-mini while racing both Winchester Speed Park and NEMX. I currently race both SX and MX at WSP. I will also try to qualify for Loretta Lynn Area Qualifier at WSP.
